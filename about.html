About Us

GWorks Construction, founded in 2016, is a leading real estate company based in Tunis, Tunisia. With a team of 51-200 professionals, we are an independent company dedicated to delivering high-quality construction projects across the region.

Our History

Founded in 2016, GWorks Construction has grown into a trusted name in the real estate industry, specializing in building projects that shape communities across Tunisia. Over the years, we’ve completed numerous residential, commercial, and industrial projects, earning a reputation for excellence and reliability.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction solutions that prioritize quality, sustainability, and client satisfaction, contributing to the development of Tunisia’s infrastructure.

Our Vision

To be the leading construction company in Tunisia, recognized for innovative designs, sustainable practices, and a commitment to building a better future for our communities.

Our Team

Our team of 51-200 professionals includes experienced engineers, architects, and project managers dedicated to delivering outstanding results. Led by our founder, Mr. Ahmed Gharbi, we bring expertise and passion to every project.

Certifications

GWorks Construction is committed to quality and sustainability. We are proud to hold certifications such as ISO 9001:2015 for Quality Management and ISO 14001:2015 for Environmental Management (pending confirmation).
